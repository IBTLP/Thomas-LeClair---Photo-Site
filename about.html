About

I’m Thomas LeClair — a photographer drawn to light, contrast, and the quiet moments that often go unnoticed. My work spans automotive, landscape, and wildlife photography, but the common thread is intention: understanding the subject, the environment, and the moment before pressing the shutter.

I approach photography as a balance between preparation and patience. Some images are planned down to the smallest detail; others require waiting, observing, and letting the scene unfold naturally. In both cases, the goal is the same — to create photographs that feel considered rather than incidental.

Based in southern Utah, I work throughout the West and travel for commissioned assignments. Whether photographing machines built for motion, landscapes shaped by time, or wildlife on their own terms, I aim to produce work that holds up — visually and materially — over the long term.

All fine art prints are produced individually on metal, with decisions around size, surface, and mounting made specifically for each piece and the space it will live in.
